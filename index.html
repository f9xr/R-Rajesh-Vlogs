The Journey

My story behind the steering wheel.

My Story

I come from humble beginnings in Jamtara, Jharkhand. For over 25 years, I have been driving trucks across India. It wasn't always easy. I started with a daily wage of just ₹150. It was a struggle to make ends meet, but I never gave up on my responsibilities.

My life changed when I started recording my daily routine and cooking in the truck cabin just to show my children. My sons saw the potential and we started R Rajesh Vlogs. Today, thanks to your love and support, I have moved from that ₹150 daily wage to earning ₹5–10 lakh monthly.

This success has allowed me to fulfill my lifelong dream—building a brand new home for my family. My mission is simple: to show the world that truck drivers are the backbone of this country and deserve respect.

How did it begin?

The channel started organically when I began recording snippets of my travels and truck cabin cooking. My first viral moment was a "face reveal" video that garnered 4.5 lakh views in a single day.

The Mission

I use my platform to destigmatize the trucking profession, emphasizing dignity and family values. I advocate for "driver-heroes" who are the backbone of the economy. My sons—Sagar, Saurabh, and Shubham—manage the digital empire.

"My life is like a heavy-duty engine that was once struggling on a steep uphill climb but has now found a high-tech turbocharger; I am still on the same road, but I am now moving with a power and prestige I never thought possible."
